Who we are

We are a dedicated nonprofit organization, committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of undeserved children, the homeless, victims of domestic violence and nursing mothers.

Our Mission

To feed the poor and needy, provide educational support, care for children and nursing mothers, empower individuals and foster positive change.

Our Vision

Through our various initiatives, we strive to eradicate hunger, reduce children mortality, improve access to education, provide essential resources, raise awareness about critical issues.

About us

Making a difference together

Imajj Foundation is a dedicated nonprofit organization, committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of undeserved children, the homeless, victims of domestic violence and nursing mothers.

We develop sustainable solutions to nourish the hungry, support education, care for children and nursing mothers, empower individuals, and inspire positive change in our communities. We invite you to learn more about our work and join us in our mission to create a brighter future for those in need. Together, we can make a significant impact!
